Dragon Ball

A young boy named Son Goku lives alone after the death of his adoptive grandfather. One day he meets Bulma, a young girl from the city and they both decide to go in search of the Dragon Balls. Once the 7 Dragon Balls are gathered, they make a dragon named Shenron appear, which fulfills any of our wishes. Goku will make many friends and will have to fight the strongest threats in the Universe if he wants to collect the 7 Dragon Balls. The manga and the anime are composed of 3 and 4 parts respectively. The last part of the Dragon Ball GT anime is not available in manga.
