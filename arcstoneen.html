    Saga Prologue

  • Petrification formula arc:

    One day, humanity found itself petrified. After 3700 years in stone, Senku Ishigami and Taiju Oki managed to get out. The two men and friends will have to survive in nature.

  • Arc of the Battle against Tsukasa:

    The two friends woke up another man while they were trapped: Tsukasa. But the latter reveals the plan he has in mind to purify the Earth of bad people. So Senku, Taiju and Yuzuriha abandon Tsukasa and try to get their hands on the miraculous water.

    • Ishigami Village Saga

  • Kingdom of Science arc

    After Taiju and Yuzuriha infiltrate the Tsukasa Kingdom, Senku encounters a man named Kohaku and a village. To gain the trust of the village, which won't let him in, Senku will then build a Kingdom of Science with the help of the village sorcerer.

  • Village Games Arc

    To get the last ingredient that would allow Senku to finish the medicine and save Ruri, the Kingdom of Science then participates in the village games.

  • Village Origins Arc

    Senku managed to save Ruri, who tells him about the Origins of the village.

    • Stone Wars Saga

  • Arc of the Battle against Hyoga

    As Senku has taken over the Ishigami village, he is attacked by Hyoga, so Senku will have to defend himself.

  • Stone Wars

    Senku and the village have succeeded in creating the first mobile phone! Thanks to him, they will be able to recontact Taiju and Yuzuriha who have already infiltrated for 1 year.

    • Source of Petrification Saga

  • Age of Exploration arc

    After realizing that the source of the petrification comes from the other side of the Earth, Senku must find a way to build a boat to cross the sea.

  • Treasure Island Arc

    To obtain rare minerals such as platinum, the Kingdom of Science stops on the treasure island but the village located on the island uses a petrification device.
