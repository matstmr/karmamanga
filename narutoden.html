Naruto

This manga is a shônen that tells the story of Naruto, a young boy who lives in the village of Konoha and who dreams of becoming Hokage. Unfortunately, a very dangerous demon has been sealed in him from an early age and is being rejected by everyone. Finally, he will manage to make some friends and the manga will dictate his adventures. The anime and the manga are composed of 2 parts, "Naruto" as well as "Naruto Shippuden". The first one part consists of 220 episodes and the second part of 500 episodes. The main characters are: Naruto, Sasuke, Sakura and Kakashi.
