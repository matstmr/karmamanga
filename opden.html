One piece

The Straw Hat Pirates are a pirate crew from East Blue, although their members come from different regions. They are the main focus and the protagonists from the One Piece anime and manga, and are led by the main protagonist, Monkey D. Luffy. The "Straw Hats" are named after Luffy's Straw Hat, which was given to him by Shanks the Roux, and are first called "Straw Hat Pirates" by Smoker in Alabasta. The crew sailed on the Vogue Merry, their first pirate crew ship, to the Arc Water Seven. After Arc Enies Lobby, they received a new ship, the Thousand Sunny. The crew is currently composed of 10 members whose total bounty is 3,161,000,100 berries. Following past events on The Sabaody Archipelago, the nine members were separated from each other. For two years, they trained in relatively isolated places, becoming stronger in order to help each other. They then reunited and became an active and massive force in the New World.
