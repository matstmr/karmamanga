    Attack on Titan arcs:

  • Shiganshina Bow Fall:

    The story begins when a 50m tall titan called the "colossal titan" breaks through the wall Maria, destroys the entire city inside the wall and threatens the future of mankind. Eren Jaeger, Mikasa Ackermann and Armin Arlelt, present in the city during the disaster, saw for themselves eyes the atrocity of the titans.

  • Battle of Trost District Arc:

    This arc takes place shortly after the graduation of Eren, Mikasa, Armin and the rest of the members of the 104th Brigade, they face another fight against the Colossal Titan when this one appears in the Trost district and goes through the wall. During this battle, Eren realizes that he too can turn into a Titan.

  • 104th Training Corps arc:

    The third arc tells how Eren, Mikasa and Armin, after witnessing the destruction and of death caused by the Titans, decide to enlist in the army as members of the 104th brigade. There they receive rigorous training to be able to fight against the Titans and do face to the difficulties of a soldier.

  • The Female Titan Arc:

    In this fourth arc, Eren is assigned to Levi's Brigade as a member of the Battalion of Exploration, directed by Erwin Smith. With his friends, they prepare for the next expedition beyond the walls. During the expedition, all members of the Exploration Battalion face several unpredictable dangers.

  • Clash of the Titans arc:

    In this game, it all starts with a group of Titans suddenly appearing at inside of the Walls, resulting in a breach somewhere in the Pink Wall. Members of the 104th brigade have to face a bloody and steep battle.

  • The Uprising Arc:

    In this sixth Arc, the battle has cost too many lives and the situation has become critical. Moreover, the story follows several political issues that begin to occur. Minister Nick is murdered and there is an attempt to kidnap Eren and Historia. Erwin recognizes that the measures taken by the king put humanity in danger, he decides to launch a plan to overthrow the government and find peace.

  • Back to Shiganshina arc:

    After a particularly turbulent sixth Arc, peace is restored within the Walls. the Exploration Battalion again prepares another expedition to Shiganshina. With the help of the news weapon created by Hange Zoe, hopes of reclaiming lost territories are stronger than never. However, in the place Eren and the others used to call home, there are enemies known and powerful that await them.

  • Marley Bow:

    In this eighth arc, the nation beyond the sea, Marley, ends their fight against the forces allies from the Middle East with the help of the power of a Titan. Nevertheless, they begin to realize that their Titan powers are losing ground in the modern age. Therefore, in order to maintain its supremacy in the world, they must take back the power of the original Titan. Unfortunately for them, the original power of the Titan is still held by Eren Jaeger and Reiner will have to face it a times of more.

  • War for Paradise Arc:

    The ninth and final arc of Attack on Titan. The Survey Battalion has returned to Paradis after Liberio's attack led by Eren. However, they find themselves in a problematic situation little long after arriving there when a faction of soldiers attempt to stage a rebellion and take control of the territory. Their goal is to found a new eldian empire with Eren as chief. Also during this time, Marley prepares his destructive counterattack on Paradis. With from enemies and internal and external conflicts, the destiny of Paradis is in great danger.
