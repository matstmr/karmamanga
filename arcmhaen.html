    My Hero Academia arcs:

  • Entrance Exam Arc:

    Izuku Midoriya is a child born without special powers called “quirks” whereas 80% of the population have them. Despite this, he always wanted to become a hero. It was then that he met his idol All Might, the greatest hero. He then decides to take him under his wing so that he can master his Quirk to enter the school of heroes.

  • Quirk Apprehension Test Arc:

    After entering the school, students in class A-1 may be expelled. They must pass a test to correctly use their quirks to know if they are fit to become heroes or not.

  • Combat Trial Arc:

    The costumes for the apprentice heroes are complete! The students are then separated into teams of two and compete in a special room. Izuku must face his friend and rival, Bakugo.

  • Arc USJ:

    As the students of Class A-1 practice at the Joint, the league of villains show up and have to fight real villains for the first time.

  • UA Sports Festival Arc:

    During this arc, the students will participate in a tournament to be spotted by the pro heroes in order to join their teams. Izuku must give everything to get spotted.

  • Hero Slayer Bow:

    Under the responsibility of the pros, Izuku and his gang must go to the field. However, a confrontation takes place between Izuku, Todoroki and Tenya Ida against a very strong villain.

  • Final Exams Arc:

    As the term comes to an end, students must take the practical and written tests. But it doesn't go as planned, they have to face their teachers in battle to pass the practical exam.

  • Forest Training Camp arc:

    During their summer vacation, the students go to a training camp to improve their skills, but the fearsome villains attack them.

  • Hideout Raid Arc:

    Following the battle at the training center, Bakugo has been kidnapped. The order of the heroes is pointed out, they will then, with the help of the police, go find Bakugo.

  • Provisional Hero License Exam Arc:

    Class A-1 students need to practice again to be ready to take the Hero License Exam.

  • Shie Hassaikai bow:

    During the second semester, the students must find an internship, so Izuku asks the office of Sir Nighteye, a former colleague of All Might.

  • Remedial Class arc:

    Following Bakugo and Todoroki's failed exam, the two attend remedial classes. At the same time, Endeavor talks to All Might very seriously.

  • UA School Festival Arc:

    As the school festival is about to begin, a man named Danjuro Tobita and his girlfriend want to destroy UA.

  • Arc Pro Hero:

    Hero Rankings is a highly anticipated event. Every two years, the ranking is updated. Endeavor took the number one spot. He barely had time to breathe for another Nomu attack.

  • Joint Formation Arc:

    During this arc, the A-1 and B-1 classes clash. During this battle, Izuku discover a power over One For All that none of the other predecessors had ever noticed.

  • Liberation Army meta arc:

    All For One and his subordinates challenged Tomura. He must prove himself worthy of taking All For One's place. At the same time, the Meta Liberation Army wants to trap the league of villains.

  • Endeavor Agency Arc:

    To enter the number one hero's agency, you have to be the best, or rather in the top three. Indeed, the three best students of class A-1 have the privilege of working under his orders.

  • Paranormal Liberation War Arc:

    With a meeting to gather information, the heroes are ready to fight against the paranormal liberation front.

  • Escaped from Tartarus arc:

    In order to prepare for the final battle against All For One, Izuku leaves UA and works with All Might and some heroes.

  • Starry and Striped Bow:

    As All Might decides to call on the foreign heroes to help them, Tomura fights back and must do everything to fulfill his master's will.

  • Current arc:

    Preparations for the final fight to definitively eliminate All For One as well as the league of villains begin and things will quickly get complicated for our heroes.
