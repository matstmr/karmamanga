    During this arc, the main characters are introduced and introduced to us. we go get to know Naruto and all his comrades. We will also attend the first mission of the famous team 7.

    We are now going to get to know all the secondary characters of the manga, and we let's follow the fights of the Chûnin exam. Sasuke obtains the cursed mark of Orochimaru

    Gaara's intense fight against Naruto. Gaara and Naruto's demons are used. Orochimaru manages to kill the third hokage.

    The Akatsuki is unveiled, the sole purpose is to steal and control the fox demon a nine tails of Naruto. Naruto, meanwhile, will undergo training to master a new technical. On their side, Jirya and Tsunade fight together against Orochimaru.

    A new mission for our team 7! They must escort a member of the Wasabi family in a race known to be dangerous because the rival family has hired their own ninjas to sabotage the race.

  • Sasuke's Salvage Mission Arc

    Orochimaru kidnapped Sasuke, so Naruto and Sakura accompanied by Jiraiya will do everything to go get it back. They will then go to Orochimaru's lair. Naruto will be able test new techniques. Orochimaru does everything to be able to make Sasuke the next one host of the fruit of his experiences.

  • Paddy Land Investigation Arcs

    Once Naruto has recovered from his battle against Sasuke, he still wants to save him from Orochimaru and goes on an adventure with Sakura. Jiraiya stumbles upon them at the Konoha border and goes on a mission with them. Once in the village of Oto, Naruto and his team cross paths with Sasame, a kunoichi. Sasame leads everyone to Orochimaru's lair.
    The battles ensue and Naruto is saved by Sakura and Sasame. Orochimaru's hideout collapses and the team learns that Orochimaru is doing everything to make Sasuke a perfect host.

    This arc is a flashback to Kakashi when he was still a child. A few years ago, the war between the ninja countries was constantly attacked. Kakashi and his friends were sent to the Land of Earth to destroy a bridge, we learn the origin of Kakashi's Sharingan and the secret of the scar on his eye.

    This arc marks the start of Part 2 of the series. We will see Deidara and his companion from the Akatsuki Sasori who go on a mission to Suna Village to capture the demon has a tail, Shukaku. We will see Sakura and Chiyo fight against Sasori and his puppets. Meanwhile, Naruto and Kakashi fight against the terrible Deidara.

    This arc marks the dispatch of Team 7 to find Sasuke. The latter is looking for his brother Itachi. He will then fight against Orochimaru and Deidara. We are going to see the legendary fight of Jiraiya against Pain and Sasuke against Itachi. The mysteries of the Uchiha clan are revealed.

The Akatsuki is looking for Jūbi's receptacle and Tobi will take care of this task. Tobi will otherwise show his face and we will learn his true identity.
